What does our website do ?

Our website is a text to ASCII Art Generator that works using the Golang language.

How does it works ?

  • Display on screen
    • Enter your text in the text area on the Generator. (Only printable ASCII characters of the ASCII table can works)
    • Then, select the font of your text. (standard, thinkertoy or shadow)
    • Moreover, you can add a color to your text.
    • Also choose the backgroud color
    • And the size of the text
    • Finally, hit the "Go convert" button
  • Export to ...
    • Same as before but the result will we downloaded to your computer
    • Please note that only TXT exportation is working for now
    • Please note that TXT exportation can only save font configuration
